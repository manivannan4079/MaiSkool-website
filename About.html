About

School Profile

Founded in 1989, the MaiSkool, Pudukkotai, is a Co-education Day School offering instruction in the English Medium as per the syllabus prescribed by the Government of INDIA for students in the Kindergarten to XII standard. The school is designed with an avowed intention of providing a high quality education and producing meritorious results in the X, XI and XII Public Examinations.


Our Founder and the Management


The school was founded by MANI.M, a businessman and philanthropist connected with several charitable institutions. A team of management experts manages the school with the help of talented administrators. The main motto of the management is to impart to the Students nug gets of wisdom and to make them imbibe life values such as punctuality and discipline.
